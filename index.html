Timeline of the Series
Doctor Who timeline from beginning.

    Here is the actors timeline:

  • 1963-1966: William Hartnell
  • 1966-1969: Patrick Troughton
  • 1970-1974: Jon Pertwee
  • 1974-1981: Tom Baker
  • 1981-1984: Peter Davison
  • 1984-1986: Colin Baker
  • 1987-1989: Sylvester McCoy
  • 1996: Paul McGann
  • 2005-2005: Christopher Eccleston
  • 2005-2010: David Tennant
  • 2010-2013: Matt Smith
  • 2013-2017: Peter Capaldi
  • 2017-present: Jodie Whittaker
